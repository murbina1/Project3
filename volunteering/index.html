FRC Midwest Regional Planning Committee

FIRST, For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology, is a robotics program for K-12 students. There are four different leagues for different age groups, Jr. FLL, FLL, FTC, and FRC. FRC, FIRST Robotics Competition, is the high school division and they make 120-pound robots.

Member of the FRC Midwest Planning Committee. Coordinated and connected with teams to train student ambassadors and create shifts at the regional. Assist the volunteer coordinator with tasks from giving out awards to interacting with VIP guests. Organized, planned, and coordinated the 2018 Chicago Kickoff Event for over 30 regional teams and 400 attendees to watch the game reveal.

Mentoring

Previous Mentor for three years of FRC Team #5125 Hawks on the Horizon team in Chicago, IL. In Pitsburgh, PA, Mentored six FIRST Lego League and Jr. FLL teams for two years.

Author of From member to mentor: Life after being a FIRST student published on Robohub.org.
